Kit's Recommendation

For the badge section next to Thumbtack: Badge #3 (Top Performer - White) or #5 (Five-Star Talent - White). The site has a dark background with purple accents, so the white variants will pop without clashing. They're the same square-ish shape as the Thumbtack badge, so they'll sit side-by-side cleanly.

Best of the two: I'd lean #3 (Top Performer - White) - "Top Performer" is a stronger trust signal than "Five-Star Talent" at a glance, and the shield shape pairs well with the Thumbtack badge.

For the reviews page: Badge #6 (Read My Reviews - Dark) could work well on the /reviews page as a CTA linking to GigSalad reviews. Different shape so it doesn't compete with the square badges.

Skip: The blue variants (#1, #2, #4) would clash with the site's purple palette. The light review banner (#7) won't work on dark backgrounds.
